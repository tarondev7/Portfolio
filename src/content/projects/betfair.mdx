Betfair Exchange is the world's largest sports betting exchange, allowing users to bet against each other rather than against a traditional bookmaker. This peer-to-peer betting model often results in better odds for bettors.

In addition to sports betting, Betfair offers online casino games, poker, and bingo, providing a comprehensive online gambling experience.
